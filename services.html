Our Services

At Omnimarket, we're committed to providing more than just great products. Our range of services is designed to enhance your shopping experience and make your culinary journey as enjoyable as possible.

  • Convenient Online Shopping • User-friendly website and mobile app • Easy search and filter options • Secure payment processing
  • Flexible Delivery Options • Same-day delivery for orders placed before noon • Scheduled deliveries • Click-and-collect from selected locations
  • Subscription Services • Weekly or monthly produce boxes • Customizable recurring orders • Special discounts for subscribers
  • Recipe Inspiration • Weekly curated recipe suggestions • Ingredient bundles for featured recipes • Cooking tips from our in-house chefs
  • Personalized Recommendations • AI-powered product suggestions based on your preferences • Tailored email notifications about products you might like
  • Gift Services • Curated gift baskets • Digital gift cards • Corporate gifting options
  • Expert Customer Support • Live chat assistance • Phone and email support • FAQ and help center
  • Nutrition Consultation • Free basic nutrition advice • Paid one-on-one sessions with registered dietitians
  • Cooking Classes • Online cooking workshops • In-person classes at select locations (post-pandemic)
  • Sustainability Program • Eco-friendly packaging options • Carbon-neutral delivery choices • Information on our sustainability initiatives
  • Special Diet Support • Filtering options for various diets (vegan, keto, gluten-free, etc.) • Nutritional information for all products
  • Community Engagement • Customer recipe sharing platform • Local producer spotlights • Community events and tastings (when safe to do so)

    • We're constantly working to improve our services. If there's something you'd like to see that we don't currently offer, please let us know!
