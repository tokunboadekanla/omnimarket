About OmniMarket

Welcome to FreshMart, your premier destination for top-quality food products delivered straight to your door.

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by food lovers Tokunbo A. and Michael G., Omnimarket was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves access to fresh, high-quality ingredients without the hassle of navigating crowded supermarkets.

Our Mission

At Omnimarket, we're committed to revolutionizing the way you shop for food. We aim to provide a curated selection of the finest local and international products, making gourmet cooking accessible to all, while supporting sustainable farming practices and ethical sourcing.
